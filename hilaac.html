Hilaac

The Glow of the Somali Horizon

"Hilaac is not just an organization; it is a rupture. A flash of lightning over a landscape often viewed through the narrow lens of the past."

Hilaac is a vanguard for radical Somali political, philosophical, and artistic thought. We stand at the intersection of ancestral lineage and speculative future, re-imagining the Somali experience outside of colonial frameworks.

Lineage

Honouring the nomadic intellectual tradition and the oral archives of our elders.

Interrogation

Critical inquiry into the structures of power, identity, and global futurisms.

Reclamation

Defining a new aesthetic and political vocabulary for the Somali vanguard.

The Forum & The Journal

Through our biennial symposia and public forums, we foster a space for rigorous debate and creative alchemy. We believe in the power of the collective intellect to bypass traditional gatekeepers.

The Hilaac Journal — launching in 2026 — will serve as the physical archive of this movement. A home for visionary poetics, deep-form essays, and the blueprints of our future.

Call for Submissions

Volume I: The First Flash (Hilaac)

The inaugural issue of the Hilaac Journal seeks work that interrogates the Somali condition through the lens of radical futurisms. We are looking for the "ruptures"—the ideas that break through the noise of the present to illuminate a path forward.

  • Poetics Speculative verse, nomadic oralities, and experimental forms.
  • Inquiry Critical essays on Somali philosophy, decoloniality, and political futurism.
  • Visuals Architectural blueprints, digital art, and photographic stills of the Somali avant-garde.

Submissions Open: Autumn 2026
