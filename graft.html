Graft

Academic, Editorial & Pedagogical Praxis

Research & Peer Review
PhD Researcher examining the psychological and structural dimensions of archetypes within the creative process. Serves as a Peer Reviewer for Neurodiversity (SAGE Journal), contributing to the critical evaluation of neurodivergent scholarship.
Instruction & Facilitation
Lead Instructor for Neurodiversity & Creative Practice and Archetypes and the Creative Process. Facilitator of Decolonised Creativity seminars, focused on dismantling colonial pedagogical frameworks to foster radical artistic autonomy.
Mentorship
Specialist NMH Mentor, providing tailored psychological and academic support for neurodivergent students and professionals in higher education and creative industries.
Editorial Oversight
Poetry Editor for flipped eye press. Curatorial lead in identifying, developing, and advocating for innovative voices in contemporary global poetics.
Advocacy
Community Organiser dedicated to grassroots structural reform and the intersection of social justice and the creative arts.
