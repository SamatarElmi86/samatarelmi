Samatar Elmi

Samatar Elmi is a poet, musician, and researcher whose work navigates the complex intersections of mythology, neurodiversity, and the Somali diaspora. As an artist operating across multiple registers, he explores the "ruptures" in cultural narratives and the resilience of the human soul.


His literary output, including The Epic of Cader Idris and Portrait of Colossus, has been widely acclaimed for its linguistic acuity and philosophical depth. Performing as Knomad Spock, he fuses nomadic oral traditions with experimental jazz and hip-hop sensibilities.


He is currently based in the UK, where he continues to develop Hilaac and the Graft pedagogical frameworks.
