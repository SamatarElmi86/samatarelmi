Samatar Elmi is an award-winning writer, musician, and educator.

His debut collection, The Epic of Cader Idris (flipped eye press, 2024), marks a significant contribution to contemporary poetry.

As Knomad Spock, his albums have been met with critical acclaim and featured on BBC Radio 6.
